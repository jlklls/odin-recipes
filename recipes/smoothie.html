Spinach and Kale Smoothie

How to cook Spinach and Kale Smoothie

This versatile kale and spinach smoothie is a delicious way to add more veggies to your diet! Chia seeds are optional but will give you added protein and energy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 leaf kale
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (Optional)
  • 1 sliced frozen banana

Steps

  1. Combine spinach, almond milk, kale, peanut butter, and chia seeds in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add banana and blend until smooth.
