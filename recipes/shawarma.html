Shawarma

How to cook shawarma

If you like a hearty and tasty snack, then this recipe for shawarma with chicken and vegetables will definitely appeal to you. Shawarma is prepared at home quite quickly.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken fillet – 500 g
  • Tomato – 200 g
  • Cucumber – 150 g
  • Salad iceberg – 150 g
  • Lavash – 4 pcs.
  • Mayonnaise – 100 g
  • Hard cheese
  • Onion (if you like)

Steps

  1. Take the chicken fillet, rinse it and cut it into thin long strips.
  2. Put the meat in the pan and fry it on middle heat setting.
  3. Cover the pan with a lid to completely fry the meat. Fry it until it has a golden crust. Turn the heat down. After that, let it cool
  4. Then chop vegetables. Grate the cheese with a grater.
  5. Then, take a pita and put the leaf of iceberg lettuce. Also put fried chicken and vegetables and sprinkle with the cheese.
  6. Now add mayo and wrap it. That is it, shawarma is ready. Enjoy!
